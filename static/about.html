ABOUT

Hi, I’m Tyler, and I love designing, building, and implementing cool ideas. I’m studying software engineering at the University of Portsmouth, which has significantly impacted how I develop apps, especially in Node JS. I’ve also been trained to use PostgreSQL, Python, the fundamentals of rust, and some of the .net languages (VB and C#).

TYWAY is where I can self-host my apps and show off what I’ve built; for example, in my first year of uni, I made a hangman web app which I now host at hangman.tyway.net. I plan to add many more web apps but not only that, I love developing local applications and programs, which I also plan to show off on this platform. Making things for TYWAY is something I love to do because this is where I can apply what I’ve learned and experiment with other technologies.

Thanks for visiting, and be sure to check out my collection of apps!
